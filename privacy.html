Privacy Policy

StriveUp respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how your data is handled when you use the StriveUp app.

Data Collection

StriveUp does not collect, store, or share any personal data on external servers. All goals, habits, and progress data are stored locally on your device or within your personal iCloud account, if enabled.

Notifications

StriveUp uses notifications to remind you of your goals. Notification permissions can be managed at any time in your device settings.

Third-Party Services

StriveUp does not use third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Data Sharing

Your data is never sold or shared with third parties.
