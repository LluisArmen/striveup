Frequently Asked Questions

What makes StriveUp different from other goal apps? ‚ñº
StriveUp stands out with its flexible frequency system (daily, weekly, monthly, yearly), beautiful visual design with progress rings and flame streaks, and smart recurring notifications that adapt to your goal timeline. Plus, it's completely free with zero ads.
Can I track goals on different timeframes? ‚ñº
Yes! StriveUp lets you create goals for any frequency: daily (like meditation), weekly (like workouts), monthly (like savings), or yearly (like travel adventures). Each goal adapts to its own period.
How do notifications work? ‚ñº
Enable notifications on any goal and choose when you want to be reminded: minutes, hours, days, or months before your period ends. Notifications automatically renew each period, so you'll never miss a goal deadline.
What's a streak and why does it matter? ‚ñº
A streak is the number of consecutive periods you've completed your goal. Streaks build motivation and consistency. Watch your flame üî• grow as you maintain your habits. Every day or week you complete counts toward building an unstoppable streak.
Is StriveUp free? ‚ñº
Yes! StriveUp is completely free with all core features included: unlimited goals, all frequencies, smart reminders, streak tracking, and more. No ads, no paywalls. A premium tier is coming soon with advanced features.
What happens when I complete a goal? ‚ñº
When you reach your target for the period (e.g., 3 workouts per week), the goal is marked complete. Your streak increases. At the end of the period, everything resets and you can start fresh. Notifications are automatically rescheduled for the new period.
Can I use StriveUp offline? ‚ñº
Yes! StriveUp works completely offline. Your data is stored locally on your device. All tracking, progress updates, and streak counting work without an internet connection.
Is my data private? ‚ñº
Your data is 100% private. StriveUp never collects personal information or sends data to external servers. Everything stays on your device. We respect your privacy completely.
